#### Conditional Probability
$$
\begin{aligned}
P\{ A | B \} = \frac{P\{A \cap B\}}{P\{B\}}
\end{aligned}
$$

#### Bayes's Theorem
$$
\begin{aligned}
P\{ A | B \} = \frac{P\{B | A\} * P\{A\}}{P\{B\}}
\end{aligned}
$$
